Regret Optimizer
================

The :class:`RegretOptimizer` inherits the :class:`DiscreteUncertaintyOptimizer`. The `minimum regret optimization <https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Regret_(decision_theory)>`_ is a technique under decision theory on making decisions under uncertainty.
